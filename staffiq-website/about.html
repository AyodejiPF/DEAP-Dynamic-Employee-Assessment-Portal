Our story

Built by consultants who know the SME struggle

StaffiQ comes from RevenStrat Integrated Services, a Lagos based business management consultancy. We spent decades in the room with African SMEs, and we turned what we learned into a product any team can use.

Why we built it

Your biggest problem is rarely the one you think

Across years of work with business owners, one pattern kept repeating. Organisations treated the symptom, not the cause. They blamed people when the real gap was training, and they spent on training with no dependable way to assess understanding.

StaffiQ exists to close that loop. Teach the skill, assess understanding, then show leadership the evidence. It is root cause thinking built into practical software.

  • Decades of hands on SME consulting across Nigeria and Africa
  • Courses distilled from real client work, not textbooks
  • Built for local networks, local budgets and local realities
  • A consulting team behind the software when you want a human
What we believe

The principles behind the product

Find the root cause

We fix the real problem, not the loud one. Every dashboard is designed to point you at the cause, not just the symptom.

Evidence over ego

Promotions, training and tough calls should rest on data everyone can see, so decisions are fair and easy to defend.

Built for here

Mobile aware, plain spoken and grounded in the realities of African SMEs.
