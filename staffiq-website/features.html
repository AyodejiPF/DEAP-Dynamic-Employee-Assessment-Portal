Pillar 01

Training that fits a busy day

Short, practical courses your team can take between tasks, plus the tools to build your own from your playbooks.

  • Soft skills library: communication, leadership, project management, critical thinking and more
  • Bite size lessons with completion ticks and instant explanations
  • Playback speed controls and recently viewed content
  • Custom courses built from your own procedures and product knowledge
  • Estimated reading time so staff can plan around a shift
Pillar 02

Assessment that feels fair

A structured assessment experience with clear progress, practical controls and useful results.

  • Timed and untimed practice modes with instant explanations
  • Question flagging and a pre submit review so nobody submits blanks
  • Countdown warnings and a kind auto submit when time expires
  • Plain language result: you passed, your strongest topic, your next focus
  • Result records available for authorised review
Pillar 03

Analytics leaders act on

The least useful slice of assessment data is a pass rate. StaffiQ goes deeper so you fix the right thing.

  • Dashboard summaries for activity and results
  • Question and topic performance analysis
  • User and assessment result comparisons
  • Searchable reports for authorised administrators
  • Plain language intervention signals
Pillar 04

An experience people finish

Adoption is won or lost on the small things. StaffiQ sweats them.

  • Responsive layouts for phone, tablet and desktop
  • Mobile bottom navigation and big, tappable answer cards
  • Persistent progress and clear assessment navigation
  • Keyboard access, visible focus and reduced motion support
  • Guided first login tour and friendly, plain error messages
Pillar 05

Security and governance built in

Serious controls for organisations that answer to auditors, boards and regulators.

  • Role based permissions across employee and administrator access
  • Searchable governance and audit records
  • Server enforced controls for privileged actions
  • Token governance with rotation, risk and an expiry calendar
  • Protected administrative and account data paths
