# Evidence Research Synthesis

Use this skill when decisions must be grounded in evidence rather than opinion.

## Workflow

1. Clarify the decision the research must support.
2. Gather current sources when the topic may have changed.
3. Separate facts, opinions, assumptions, and inferences.
4. Compare evidence across at least two source types when possible.
5. Identify the root cause, not only the visible symptom.
6. Turn research into options, trade offs, recommendation, and action.
7. State uncertainty clearly.

## Business Lens

For Nigerian SMEs, connect research to cash flow, staffing, customer behaviour, regulation, adoption difficulty, owner psychology, and implementation cost.

Avoid long research dumps that do not change a decision.

## Output Format

Use these sections:

Question, evidence, interpretation, options, recommendation, risks, action plan.
