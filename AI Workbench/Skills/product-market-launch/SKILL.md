# Product Market Launch

Use this skill to move from product building to customer acquisition and revenue.

## Workflow

1. Define the buyer, user, payer, blocker, and influencer.
2. State the painful problem in plain business language.
3. Define the offer and the strongest measurable outcome.
4. Create the proof plan: demo, case story, screenshots, testimonials, pilot results, or savings estimate.
5. Define the pricing logic and package structure.
6. Build the sales path: lead source, first message, discovery call, demo, proposal, close, onboarding.
7. Create objections and responses.
8. Map launch actions into weekly tasks.

## Nigerian SME Lens

Assume buyers may want practical value, proof, affordability, trust, simplicity, and personal reassurance before buying.

Avoid selling technology for its own sake. Sell control, visibility, reduced waste, better staff accountability, and clearer profit impact.

## Output Format

Use these sections:

Market position, buyer profile, pain points, offer, pricing, sales funnel, launch actions, proof assets, next seven days.
